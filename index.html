About Dove

Dove is a globally trusted personal care brand known for its gentle, nourishing, and dermatologically tested products. Launched in 1957, Dove has grown into one of the world’s most loved brands for skincare and haircare. With its signature 1/4 moisturizing cream formula, Dove focuses on providing real care that leaves skin soft, healthy, and hydrated.

Dove is committed to promoting *real beauty*, body positivity, and self-confidence. Through campaigns like the Dove Real Beauty initiative, the brand encourages people to embrace and celebrate who they truly are. Their products are designed for everyday use and are suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Fun Fact

The name 'Dove' (the bird) symbolizes peace and purity, reflecting the brand's gentle and clean approach to personal care.

Our Product Philosophy

Dove’s wide range includes beauty bars, body washes, shampoos, conditioners, deodorants, lotions, and baby care products. Each item is crafted with gentle ingredients and backed by dermatological expertise to ensure safe, effective care for everyone.
